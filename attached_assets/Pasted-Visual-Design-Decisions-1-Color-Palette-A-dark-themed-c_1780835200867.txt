Visual Design Decisions:
1. Color Palette
A dark-themed colour system was selected to reduce glare under Sarawak's intense sunlight. Colours follow familiar traffic-light conventions (red=danger, yellow=caution, green=safe, orange=unavailable) to support quick recognition with minimal cognitive effort. Secondary labels use 60% opacity white to create visual hierarchy.
